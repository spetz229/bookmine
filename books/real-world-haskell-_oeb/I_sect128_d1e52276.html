28.7. Communication Between Threads

As well as the basic TVar type, the stm package provides two types that are more useful for communicating between threads. A TMVar is the STM equivalent of an MVar: it can hold either Just a value or Nothing. The TChan type is the STM counterpart of Chan, and it implements a typed FIFO channel.
