Clients Make Great Targets

This chapter is at its heart a laundry list of techniques that are useful for taking apart and understanding game clients. We show you how to get in, over, under, and way outside clients using common techniques from code analysis. We even hint at kernel-mode techniques.

One of the ultimate tools for client-side game hacking is the bot, and a key component for building a bot is the debugger. Presenting a debugger is a great way to unify our foray into client-side hacking, which we do in the next chapter.
