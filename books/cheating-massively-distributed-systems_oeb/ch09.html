9. Advanced Game Hacking Fu


Now that you've come this far in the book, you've learned about client-side state, network-based sniffing, attacker-in-the-middle hacks, and several other standard techniques for hacking online games. In this chapter, we take on what's known as modding—an entertaining way to explore game hacking.
