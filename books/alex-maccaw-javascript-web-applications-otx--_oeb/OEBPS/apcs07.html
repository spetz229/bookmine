Multiple Backgrounds

Just as you can specify multiple shadows in CSS3, you can specify multiple backgrounds. Previously, to have many background images, you’d have to create a lot of nested elements—i.e., too much extraneous markup. CSS3 lets you give a comma-separated list to the background style, greatly reducing the amount of markup required:

background: url(snowflakes.png) top repeat-x,
  url(chimney.png) bottom no-repeat,
  -moz-linear-gradient(white, #CCC),
  #CCC;

Browser support:

  • Firefox: full support

  • Chrome: full support

  • Safari: full support

  • IE >= 9.0: full support

  • Opera: full support
