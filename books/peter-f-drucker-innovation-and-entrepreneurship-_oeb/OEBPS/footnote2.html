* Kondratjeff’s long-wave cycle was popularized in the West by the Austro-American economist Joseph Schumpeter, in his monumental book Business Cycles (1939). Kondratieff’s best known, most serious, and most important disciple today—and also the most serious and most knowledgeable of the prophets of “long-term stagnation”—is the MIT scientist Jay Forrester.
